Grasslands National Park

Saskatchewan

Grasslands National Park was established in 1981 and is located in the Frenchman River area in the south of Saskatchewan. The park is divided into an east block and a west block. There are not very many maps of this park, historical or recent, mainly because the terrain in the area in which this park is located is not subject to extreme environmental changes. It does however share the same similarity with every other park in this site, the maps sadly become increasingly more boring after 1990.
