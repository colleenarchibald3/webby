Riding Mountain National Park

Manitoba

About the Park

Riding Mountain National Park was established in 1929 and occupies around 2973.1 km2 of land. It is located in Manitoba near the prairie on the Manitoba Escarpment and is considered a "core protected area". Its landscape is a mix of forests and grasslands along with small lakes, streams, and bogs.

From 1960s-1990s

This is a great display of how designing maps based on where the park is can be very impactful. Obviously I find the bright and vibrant colours very intriguing but there is a clear intention with the colours being used considering this is a landlocked park near a prairie. And not only is green being used pretty much exclusively but it is being used in a very unique and visually interesting way.
