Wood Buffalo National Park

Northwest Territories

Wood Buffalo National Park was established in 1922 and covers around 44 802 km2 of land in the Northwest Territories. The parks land consists of a plateau, an inland delta, and forested uplands and was established to protect wood bison in the Northwest Territories.
