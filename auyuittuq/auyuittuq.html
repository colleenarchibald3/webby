Auyuittuq National Park

Nunavut

About the Park

Auyuittuq National Park is located on Baffin Island in Nunavut. It was established as a national park reserve in 1972 and was officially named a full national park in 2000. It was the first national park in canada to be established north of the Arctic Circle.
