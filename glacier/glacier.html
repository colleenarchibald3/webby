Glacier National Park

British Columbia

About the Park

Glacier National Park was established in 1886 and covers around 1349.3 km2 of land. It is located in British Columbia on the border of B.C. and Alberta in the Rocky Mountains, more specifically in the Glaciers of the mountains, hence the name.

Maps

From 1930s-1960s

Glacier is a unique park that happens to have most of its earlier maps in sets of two because of its proximity to other parks in British Columbia and Alberta. Again we have the super cool fine line detailed maps. Not much changes about these maps from year to year other than the colours of the bolder trail lines but I feel like it's a very cool way to modify the maps even though the details of the land didn't change.
