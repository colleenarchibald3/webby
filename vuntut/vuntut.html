Vuntut National Park

Yukon

Vuntut National Park was established in 1995 and is located in Yukon. It spans 4345 km2 of land as a portion of the Old Crow Plain. The north of the park borders another Canadian National Park: Ivvavik National Park and the west side of the park is on the border of Alaska. The park is only accessible by a charter aircraft or a supposedly very long canoe ride.
