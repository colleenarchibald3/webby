Cape Breton National Park

Nova Scotia

Cape Breton National Park was established in 1936 and occupies 948 km2 of land and is made up of 300 m cliffs, coves, and beaches. It is located in Nova Scotia on the East Coast of Canada and borders the Atlantic Ocean.
